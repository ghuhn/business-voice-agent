# Maneuver — Knowledge Base

## About
Maneuver is a boutique product design and strategy studio founded by Arjun Mehta.
We work with early-stage startups and growth-stage companies to design, validate,
and ship digital products that win in their market. We are not a dev shop — we are
a thinking partner who also executes.

## Services
1. **Product Discovery Sprint** — 2-week intensive to map the problem space, define
   the MVP, and produce a prioritized feature roadmap. Deliverable: Discovery Report + Roadmap.

2. **UX/UI Design** — Full product design from wireframes to high-fidelity Figma
   prototypes. Includes user research, information architecture, and design system creation.

3. **Brand & Identity** — Naming, visual identity, brand guidelines, and go-to-market
   messaging for startups building a market presence.

4. **Growth Design** — Conversion optimization, onboarding flow redesign, A/B testing
   frameworks. We work with your existing product to remove friction and accelerate growth.

5. **Fractional CPO / Design Advisory** — Arjun embeds with your team 1–2 days per week
   as a strategic design leader. Best for seed/Series A companies without a senior design hire.

## Process
1. **Intake Call** (free, 30 min) — We understand your situation, goals, and constraints.
2. **Proposal** (within 48h) — Scoped statement of work, timeline, and investment.
3. **Kickoff** — Align on success metrics, stakeholders, and communication cadence.
4. **Sprints** — 2-week sprint cycles with mid-sprint check-ins and end-of-sprint reviews.
5. **Handoff** — Full design files, documentation, and a 30-day support window.

## Pricing Model
- We do not publish fixed rates publicly — every engagement is scoped to the problem.
- Typical Discovery Sprint: $8,000–$15,000
- Typical UX/UI project: $20,000–$60,000 depending on scope and duration
- Advisory retainers: $5,000–$10,000/month
- We do NOT work on equity-only deals.
- We occasionally offer reduced rates for mission-driven startups (climate, health, education).
